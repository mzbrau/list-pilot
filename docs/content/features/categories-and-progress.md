# Categories & progress

Items in a list are grouped by category so you can scan efficiently while walking the store.

## Category headers

Each category appears as a full-width header bar with a teal gradient, showing the category name and item count — for example **Fruit & Veg (3)** or **Pantry (3)**.

![Shopping list with categories in light mode](/img/screenshot-list-light.png)

## Default categories

Built-in categories include:

- Fruit & Veg
- Meat & Fish
- Dairy
- Frozen
- Bakery
- Drinks
- Snacks
- Pantry
- Household
- And more

## Recategorizing items

Tap any item to open [Item details](/docs/features/item-details) and change its category. If you recategorize a built-in catalog item, List Pilot remembers your preference.

## Progress indicator

The app bar displays:

- **List name** — e.g. "Supermarket"
- **Progress badge** — remaining/total count (e.g. **8/10**)
- **Progress bar** — a thin teal line below the app bar

This gives you an at-a-glance view of how much shopping is left.
