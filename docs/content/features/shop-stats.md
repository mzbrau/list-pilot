# Shop Stats

**Shop Stats** times your shopping trips and compares your pace to past shops on the same list. It is designed to gamify shopping a little — see if you can beat your previous runs.

Shop Stats is **disabled by default**. Nothing is recorded until you turn it on.

## Enable Shop Stats

1. Open **Settings** from the gear icon on the main lists screen.
2. Under **Features**, turn on **Shop Stats**.

Your choice is saved locally. When disabled, the app works exactly as before — no timing, no stats page, no data recorded.

## How timing works

Shop Stats measures wall-clock time from when you check off your **first item** to when you check off your **last item** on a list.

- Stats are tracked **per list** — your Costco times are compared only to past Costco shops, not your corner store.
- Unchecking items mid-shop keeps the timer running.
- **Clear all** in "In your cart" abandons an in-progress shop without saving it.
- All data stays **on your device** — nothing is sent to the cloud.

## Live ticker

While a shop is in progress, a compact bar at the bottom of the list shows:

- **Elapsed time** — how long since your first check-off
- **Current pace** — average time per item so far
- **Delta vs average** — how your current pace compares to your long-term average for this list (green = faster, red = slower)

The ticker appears only when Shop Stats is enabled and you have at least one item still to check off.

## Shop complete summary

When you check off the last item, a summary sheet appears with:

- Total shopping time
- Time per item
- Comparison to your last shop, personal best, and all-time average
- Your shop number on this list

Tap **View all stats** to open the full history page, or **Done** to dismiss.

## Shop Stats page

When Shop Stats is enabled, a chart icon appears in the app bar on the main lists screen. Tap it to open **Shop Stats**.

The page shows:

- **Total shops** and **best pace** across all lists
- **Per-list sections** with shop count, average pace, and best pace
- **Individual shop cards** with date, duration, item count, pace, and rank badge (PB, 2nd, 3rd, etc.)

:::tip Getting started
Add a few items to a list, enable Shop Stats, then check them all off to record your first shop. Comparisons become more meaningful after your second completed shop on that list.
:::

## Privacy

Shop stats are stored in the same local SQLite database as your lists. They are never uploaded or synced. Disabling Shop Stats hides the UI but keeps existing records — re-enable anytime to see them again.

See also: [Settings & export](/docs/features/settings-and-export), [Privacy](/docs/privacy).
