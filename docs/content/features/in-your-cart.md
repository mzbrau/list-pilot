# In your cart

When you check off an item, it moves to the **In your cart** section at the bottom of the list — keeping active items visible while preserving what you've already collected.

![Dark mode list showing In your cart section](/img/screenshot-list-dark.png)

## Checked items

Completed items appear with:

- A filled teal checkbox
- Strikethrough text at reduced opacity
- Grouping under the collapsible **In your cart** header

## Collapsing the section

Tap the **In your cart** header to expand or collapse the section. This helps focus on remaining items when you're nearly done.

## Clear all

When you've finished shopping, tap **Clear all** in the In your cart header to remove all completed items at once and start fresh for your next trip.

:::tip Bulk checkout
Checking off several items quickly at checkout (within about 2 seconds of each other) is detected as bulk checkout. These events still count for learning but are weighted less heavily so they don't skew your aisle-by-aisle order.
:::
