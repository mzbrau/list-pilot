# Your first list

Getting started with List Pilot takes just a few taps.

## Create a list

1. Open List Pilot — you'll see the main lists overview.
2. Tap the **+** floating action button.
3. Enter a name (e.g. "Supermarket" or "Hardware store").
4. Tap **Create** — you'll be taken straight into your new list.

## Add items

1. Tap the search bar at the top that says **Add an item...**
2. Start typing — suggestions from the built-in catalog appear as you type.
3. Tap a suggestion to add it, or type a new name and tap **+** to add a custom item.

Custom items are saved to your personal catalog for future autocomplete.

## Check off items while shopping

Tap the checkbox next to an item when you've picked it up. Checked items move to the **In your cart** section at the bottom of the list.

## Progress tracking

The app bar shows your progress as a count (e.g. **8/10**) and a thin progress bar. This helps you see how much is left at a glance.
